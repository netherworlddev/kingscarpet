PRO AIR DUCT & VENT CLEANING

We remove the hidden dust, allergens, and dander trapped in your HVAC system to restore your home's air quality.

Clean Air Starts From Within

Your air ducts act as the "lungs" of your home. Over time, they accumulate significant amounts of dust, pet hair, mold spores, and construction debris. When your heater or AC kicks on, these contaminants are recirculated throughout your living space.

  • ✓
    Industrial Brush & Vac: Dislodges stubborn buildup from duct walls.
  • ✓
    Allergy Relief: Reduces indoor allergy symptoms and respiratory irritation.
  • ✓
    Dryer Vent Cleaning: Reduces fire hazards and improves drying times.

The Kings Air-Pure Process

1. System Inspection: We check for debris levels.
2. Negative Pressure Setup: Prevents dust from escaping into your home.
3. Agitation & Extraction: Compressed air tools "sweep" the ducts.
4. Sanitization: Antimicrobial fogging treatment available.
