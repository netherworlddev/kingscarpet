Deep Porous Restoration

TILE & GROUT REVIVAL

Stop scrubbing on your hands and knees. We blast away years of built-up grime and seal your grout for long-lasting beauty.

Bring Back the Shine

Grout is incredibly porous, acting like a sponge for mopping water, grease, and bacteria. Over time, your grout lines darken and become a breeding ground for mold. Our high-pressure steam system reaches deep into those pores to flush out contaminants that standard cleaning can't touch.

  • ✓
    High-Pressure Extraction: We use specialized "swirl" tools that contain the water while blasting grime at 1000+ PSI.
  • ✓
    Grout Sealing: After cleaning, we apply a professional-grade sealer to prevent future staining and liquid absorption.
  • ✓
    Safe for All Surfaces: Whether it's Ceramic, Porcelain, Travertine, or Slate, we have the right pH-neutral solution.

Our Professional Tile Process

1. Pre-Treatment: An alkaline-based cleaner is applied to break down grease and heavy soil.
2. Power Scrub: We use mechanical brushes to agitate the grout lines and loosen stubborn debris.
3. Turbo Wash: High-pressure hot water extraction flushes everything into our truck-mounted waste tank.
4. Protective Seal: We apply a clear penetrative sealer to keep your floors looking "just cleaned" for longer.
