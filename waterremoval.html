Emergency Response Team

WATER REMOVAL & RESTORATION

Fast-acting emergency water extraction and structural drying to protect your home from mold and permanent damage.

Minutes Matter in a Flood

Whether it’s a burst pipe, a leaking water heater, or storm damage, water can ruin your home's structure and subfloors in a matter of hours. At Kings, we provide rapid-response water removal services designed to stop damage in its tracks. Our IICRC-trained technicians use industrial-grade equipment to extract standing water and implement professional drying protocols.

  • ✓
    Industrial Extraction: We utilize truck-mounted vacuum systems that remove hundreds of gallons of water in minutes, far outperforming standard shop-vacs.
  • ✓
    Structural Drying & Dehumidification: We deploy high-velocity air movers and LGR dehumidifiers to pull moisture out of walls, floors, and the air to prevent warping.
  • ✓
    Anti-Microbial Treatment: To prevent mold and mildew growth, we apply hospital-grade botanical disinfectants to all affected surfaces.

Our Restoration Process

1. Inspection & Assessment: We use moisture meters and thermal imaging to find "hidden" water behind baseboards and under padding.
2. Water Extraction: Immediate removal of standing water to contain the damage area.
3. Drying & Dehumidifying: Continuous monitoring of humidity levels until your structure reaches "dry standard" levels.
4. Sanitization: Deep cleaning and deodorizing to ensure your home is safe, healthy, and odor-free.
